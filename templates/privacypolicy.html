Privacy Policy

At World Hotels, we are committed to protecting your privacy across our UK hotel network. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary for hotel bookings and service improvement:

  • Booking Information:
    • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
    • Check-in/check-out dates
    • Room preferences and guest numbers
    • Special requests or requirements
  • Account Information:
    • Login credentials
    • Booking history
    • Preferred currency settings
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment gateways for room bookings and cancellation charges.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage hotel bookings
  • Calculate and apply appropriate seasonal rates and advance booking discounts
  • Handle booking modifications and cancellations
  • Send booking confirmations and receipts
  • Process applicable cancellation charges
  • Maintain booking records for the maximum 3-month advance booking period

3. Data Retention

We retain your booking information for:

  • Active bookings: Until completion of your stay
  • Booking history: For financial and legal compliance
  • Account information: Until account deletion request

4. Data Security

  • Secure encryption for all booking transactions
  • Protected access to booking management systems
  • Regular security audits of our booking platform
  • Secure handling of advance booking discounts and cancellation charges

5. Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • The specific hotel where you've made a booking
  • Payment processing partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your booking information
  • Modify your personal details
  • Cancel bookings (subject to our cancellation policy)
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

We retain booking information for the duration of your stay and maintain records as required for legal and financial compliance.
