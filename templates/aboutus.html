Our Story

Our story began with a simple goal: to create a place where guests could feel at home. Founded decades ago as a small family-run hotel, we quickly became known for our warmth, comfort, and exceptional service. Nestled in the heart of the city, we provided a welcoming escape for travelers from all walks of life. Over the years, our passion for hospitality has grown, transforming us into a trusted name in the industry. While expanding our services, we've stayed true to our core values, ensuring every guest enjoys a memorable experience. We take pride in being a part of countless journeys, creating cherished memories that last a lifetime.

Travel with Purpose is World Hotels Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) strategy to drive responsible travel and tourism globally. Through Travel with Purpose, we seek to create positive environmental and social impact across our operations, supply chain, and communities.
