Terms and Conditions

Last Updated: January 21, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to World Hotels. These terms and conditions outline the rules and regulations for the use of our website and services across our hotels in the United Kingdom.

2. Room Types and Occupancy

  • Standard Room: Single occupancy only
  • Double Room: Maximum 2 guests (20% higher than standard room rate plus 10% surcharge for second guest)
  • Family Room: Maximum 4 guests (50% higher than standard room rate)

3. Seasonal Rates

Peak season rates apply during:

  • April to August (inclusive)
  • November to December (inclusive)

Off-peak rates apply during all other months.

4. Booking and Stay Duration

  • Bookings can be made up to 3 months in advance
  • Maximum stay duration is 30 days per booking
  • For stays longer than 30 days, separate bookings are required
  • All bookings are subject to room availability

5. Advance Booking Discounts

The following discounts apply based on advance booking period:

  • 30% discount: 80-90 days in advance
  • 20% discount: 60-79 days in advance
  • 10% discount: 45-59 days in advance
  • No discount: Less than 45 days in advance

6. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation charges are calculated based on the time before check-in date:

  • More than 60 days: No cancellation charge
  • 30-60 days: 50% of total booking price
  • Less than 30 days: 100% of total booking price

7. Payment and Currency

We accept payments in multiple currencies with real-time conversion rates. Base rates are in GBP (British Pounds).

8. Privacy & Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Your personal information will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy and UK data protection laws.
